I am a research scientist in Data Analytics and Intelligence Lab (DAIL), Alibaba DAMO Academy. I received my Ph.D. and B.S. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2019 and 2013, respectively, supervised by Professor Jianzhong Li and Professor Zhaonian Zou. My research interests focuses on large-scale data management and analytics, including intelligent databases (AI4DB), graph data mining and graph processing system.
